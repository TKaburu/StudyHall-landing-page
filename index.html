What inspired StudyHall?

""

When I joined Alx Africa everything was new. I would find it very difficult to understand all the new concepts especially with the tight deadlines. It wasn't until I met a group of peers through the Alx peer groups that I started to fully grasp the concept and not feel like I was the only one having a bard time understanding the concepts.

When the project started, that moment came to mind. Why not create a virtual hub for learners to learn together so they do not feel overwhelmed? A place you can go back to and not having to create a new room everytime. Slowly by slowly StudyHall was built and now we are here.

It is my hope that this application will help you in all you studies!

""
